Chicken and Rice

Description

Chicken and rice is a classic dish that is both comforting and delicious. It is made with simple ingredients, such as chicken, rice, vegetables, and spices, and can be easily customized to your liking.

Chicken and rice is a great dish for a weeknight meal or a potluck. It is also a good option for meal prep, as it can be easily made ahead of time and reheated.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 1 (10 3/4-ounce) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add the chicken and cook until browned on all sides.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  5. Stir in the diced tomatoes, soup, rice, and water.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked through.
  7. Stir in the parsley and serve.
